Bicol Express

Bicol Express is a rich, spicy, and creamy dish made with pork, shrimp, or fish cooked in coconut milk and chili peppers. It's a famous Bicolano dish that showcases the region's love for coconut milk and spice.

Ingredients:

  • 500g pork belly (cut into strips)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 3-5 long red chili peppers (sliced, adjust according to spice preference)
  • 1 tablespoon shrimp paste (bagoong)
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Saut√© onions and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the pork belly and cook until it starts to brown.
  3. Add the shrimp paste and cook for another 2 minutes to release its flavor.
  4. Pour in the coconut milk and water. Stir well to combine.
  5. Add the chili peppers. Bring to a simmer and cook until the pork becomes tender (about 20-30 minutes).
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
