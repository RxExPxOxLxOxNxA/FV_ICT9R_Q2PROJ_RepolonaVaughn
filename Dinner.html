Adobong Manok at Baboy sa Gata

Adobong Manok at Baboy sa Gata is a Bicolano twist on the classic adobo, where chicken and pork are cooked in a rich coconut milk sauce, with vinegar, soy sauce, and spices. It’s a comforting and flavorful dish that's perfect for dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 300g chicken (cut into serving pieces)
  • 300g pork belly (cut into cubes)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 can (400ml) coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat and sauté the onion and garlic until softened.
  2. Add the chicken and pork. Cook until the meat starts to brown.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce, vinegar, and bay leaf. Stir well, then cover and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the coconut milk, then simmer for another 20-30 minutes, or until the meat is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt, pepper, and a bit of sugar if you like a touch of sweetness.
  6. Serve with steamed rice.
